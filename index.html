Privacy & Trust

We built Soothe Ring to be the most private wearable you'll ever own. Here's exactly how.

Your data never leaves your device

  • Raw tactile data is processed and encrypted locally on your phone. Nothing is stored in the cloud.

  • No cloud logs, no third-party access, no analytics tracking. What you feel stays with you.

  • Even we can't read your data. Your Soothe Ring speaks only to your phone.

It only feels what you feel

  • The Soothe Ring measures tactile input on the wearer only — pressure, temperature, texture, movement.

  • It can never identify other people. It doesn't know who or what you're touching — only how it feels to you.

Cryptographically bound to you

  • Your ring is cryptographically paired to one owner's phone. No one else can connect to it.

  • Phone proximity is required for tracking to activate. If your phone isn't nearby, the ring stays silent.

  • Re-pairing to a different phone requires a full factory reset and fresh consent onboarding. No shortcuts.

No covert monitoring — ever

  • Parental access requires an active, non-dismissible notification on the child's device. They always know.

  • Parents receive wellness summaries only — like 'balanced' or 'low input today.' Never detailed touch logs.

  • Transparency is always the default. We designed this so trust flows both ways.

Our promise

Daily Tactile Input will never sell, share, or monetise your sensory data. We exist to help you understand your touch world — nothing more.
